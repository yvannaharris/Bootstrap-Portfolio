About Me

A Florida native, Yvanna Harris studied public relations and business administration at the University of Florida. Since then, Yvanna has chosen to work as a logistics and inventory manager for a major retailer.

Yvanna has recently embarked on a new journey to become a full-stack web developer with the UCF Coding Bootcamp. She hopes to pursure a career in digital marketing at the end of the course.

In her free time, Yvanna loves to take in Broadway musicals. Her favorites include; Les Miserbale, Spring Awakening, Hamilton and Dear Evan Hansen.
